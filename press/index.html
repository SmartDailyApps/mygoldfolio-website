Product Summary

MyGoldFolio is a smart, private gold portfolio tracker for Android. It combines live gold pricing, holding-level analytics, tax-free milestone tracking, and scenario tools to support informed decisions.

The app has a strong free core and an optional one-time Premium Lifetime unlock for higher limits and advanced features. Portfolio data remains on-device.

Factsheet

  • Platform: Android
  • Model: Free + optional Premium Lifetime
  • Data model: On-device first
  • Languages: EN, DE, TR, FR
